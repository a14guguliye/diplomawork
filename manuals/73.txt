General Description
The GO™ Switch Model 73 is the world’s most used and widely
trusted leverless limit switch with no external moving parts, no
springs or cams, and no reed element. There is nothing to wear or fail.
Installation
The 70 Series GO™ Switches can be installed in any
position, directly with the threaded body or through
mounting kits designed for the application.
